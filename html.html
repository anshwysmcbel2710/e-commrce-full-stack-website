🏥 The Challenge

Why Your Business Deserves More Than a Template

Generic website builders limit your brand, your growth, and your ability to stand out. Here is what you risk when using off-the-shelf platforms.

🔒

You Don't Own Your Platform

With WordPress or Shopify, you are renting space on their platform. If they change policies or pricing, your entire business is affected.

🐌

Slow Performance Loses Sales

Template websites loaded with plugins are slow. Every second of load time costs you customers — studies show 40% of users leave a website that takes more than 3 seconds.

🧩

Limited Customisation

You are forced to work within the constraints of themes and plugins. Your brand ends up looking like thousands of other websites.

💸

Hidden Monthly Costs

Shopify charges 2-3% transaction fees plus ₹2,000–20,000/month in plan fees plus paid plugins. Costs compound silently.

📉

Poor SEO Control

Generic platforms have limited SEO capabilities. Google ranks fast, custom-coded websites significantly higher than template sites.

⛔

No Scalability

As your business grows, template platforms buckle under traffic. Migrating later is painful and expensive.
